DYDownloader is an Android app for downloading watermark-free Douyin original images, galleries, live photos, videos, account posts, and collection posts.

## Overview

- Download watermark-free Douyin original images, galleries, live photos, and videos
- Parse and download post, account, and collection links
- Built on local parsing plus official Douyin interfaces and does not rely on third-party parsing platforms or relay services
- Open sourced under GPL-3.0, see [LICENSE](LICENSE)

## Cookies

Some parsing and download flows require Cookies.

- You need to set Cookies manually: `Settings -> Cookies -> Log in to Douyin`
- Cookie data is stored locally on the device only and attached only when requesting Douyin.
- Cookies are not uploaded to any third-party platform or service
