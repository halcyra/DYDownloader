DYDownloader downloads watermark-free media from Douyin share links.

It supports single posts, creator profiles, and collections, with support for photos, galleries, live photos, covers, and videos.

Features:
- Local parsing plus official Douyin interfaces
- No third-party relay or parsing service
- Batch download support for profiles and collections
- Local download history and media preview
- Optional login via the built-in WebView when a Douyin cookie is required

Notes:
- Some content requires a valid Douyin cookie provided by the user.
- Cookies stay on the device and are only attached to requests sent to Douyin.
- The app depends on Douyin, which is a proprietary network service.

Use this app only in ways that comply with local law, platform rules, and copyright requirements.
